Products

What we’re building

Rhyfinity L.C. builds AI-powered software products that combine intelligent planning, personalized recommendations, and social interaction to help users set goals and stay motivated.

Focus areas

Core product capabilities

Our products support users in goal setting, career and learning decisions, and social connection. One core initiative is an AI-based career planning and social application designed to improve decision-making and professional networking through intelligent systems.

Career Planning & Exploration
Help users explore directions, clarify goals, and make career decisions with personalized guidance.
Intelligent Conversation & Support
Provide real-time problem solving and decision support through conversational AI.
Task Planning & Breakdown
Convert long-term goals into actionable tasks with an execution loop of reminders and reflection.
Social Connection & Motivation
Enable lightweight social interaction to keep users accountable and motivated.
